Understanding the OSI Model — From User to Wire

Every time you load a webpage, send a message, or stream a video, a complex system works silently behind the scenes to make it happen.

The Open Systems Interconnection (OSI) Model breaks this process into seven distinct layers, each responsible for a specific part of communication between devices.

This interactive webpage takes you on a journey through those layers — starting from the applications you interact with and moving deeper into the physical transmission of data.

But networks aren’t just about communication — they’re also about security. At each layer, you’ll discover not only what happens, but also where vulnerabilities can arise.
